About Us

"We believe every woman’s skill holds power."
Our platform is more than just a website, it's a mission to help Nepali women recognize the value of their skills, grow them with guidance, and become financially independent. Here, you begin by identifying your skill level, whether you're just starting out, skilled but not earning, or already monetizing your craft and we connect you with the right mentor to support your journey. Your mentor will guide you in developing your craft and teach you the financial knowledge you need to succeed. Then, you can list and sell your handmade or homegrown products in our very own e-commerce marketplace.
We take only a 10–15% commission on each sale the rest is fully yours, your effort, your income.

Our Services

Skill Mentorship

Whether you're unskilled, skilled but not earning, or already earning — we match you with a mentor who will help you grow. Our mentors teach hands-on skills and provide personal guidance based on your experience level.

Financial Education

We believe income starts with the right knowledge.Get access to financial basics like budgeting, pricing your products, marketing, and saving — all taught in simple language by women, for women.

Sell Your Products

Once you're confident in your skills, start your own business! List your handmade or homegrown products in our online marketplace and reach buyers across Nepal.

Community and Support

Join a growing community of women like you. Ask questions, get feedback, and grow together. We’re more than a platform — we’re a sisterhood.
